# Company’s Performance Analysis – Power BI Project
## Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze the company’s overall sales performance across different countries and products.
The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of key business metrics such as revenue, units sold, cost, and profit.
It helps stakeholders understand where the company is performing well and where improvements can be made by breaking insights down by product, geography, and time.

## Dataset Used
<a href="https://github.com/DevGik/Data-Analysis-Project/blob/main/Power%20BI%20Exercise%202%20edited.pbix">Power BI data</a>

## Questions (KPIs) the Dashboard Answers
This report was developed to answer the following key performance questions:
1.	What is the total revenue generated?
2.	How many units were sold across all countries?
3.	What is the total cost incurred?
4.	What is the total profit earned?
5.	Which products generate the highest revenue and profit?
6.	Which countries contribute most to units sold and revenue?
7.	How does revenue vary across months?
8.	What is the geographical distribution of revenue across countries?

## Process
The following steps were carried out to build the report:
1. Data Loading
  - Imported raw data into Power BI Desktop.
  - Confirmed data structure and inspected column types.
2. Data Cleaning & Transformation (Power Query)
  - Handled missing or inconsistent values.
  - Standardized country and product names.
  - Ensured accurate data types for numerical and date fields.
  - Performed aggregations needed for KPIs.
3. Data Modeling
  - Reviewed relationships between tables (if applicable).
  - Created calculated columns and DAX measures for:
    - Total Revenue
    - Total Units Sold
    - Total Cost
    - Total Profit
4. Visualization
  - Designed a clean, user-friendly dashboard.
  - Included slicers for Country and Year to enable dynamic analysis.
  - Added KPI cards for quick overview.
  - Used bar charts, column charts, pie charts, map visual, and tables.
5. Formatting & Branding
  - Applied a consistent theme for visual clarity.
  - Used layout alignment, colors, and icons to enhance readability.

## Project Insights
The dashboard revealed the following insights:
1.	Revenue & Profit Overview
  - Total revenue: 9 million
  - Total profit: 5 million
  - The business is operating profitably across all products.
2.	Product Performance
  - Chocolate Chip is the highest performing product in both revenue and profit.
  - White Chocolate, Oatmeal Raisin, and Snickerdoodle also show strong performance.
  - Products like Sugar and Fortune Cookie contribute less profit.
3.	Geographical Insights
  - Canada and the United States account for the largest share of units sold and revenue.
  - Other countries such as France and Germany contribute moderately.
  - Regions such as India and Kenya show much smaller sales volumes.
4.	Seasonality (Revenue by Month)
  - Revenue fluctuates across months, with notable peaks in specific months (as shown in the bar chart).
  - Indicates possible seasonal buying patterns.
5.	Units Sold Distribution
  - Canada and the United States dominate the units sold share.
  - Smaller markets indicate opportunities for expansion.

## Final Conclusion
The dashboard provides a clear overview of the company’s performance across products, countries, and time.
The business is generating strong revenue and profit, with top-performing products driving most of the success.
Geographically, North America is the largest market, while other regions offer opportunities for targeted growth.
Overall, this Power BI report helps leadership quickly understand business performance, identify strengths, and spot potential areas for strategic improvement.
